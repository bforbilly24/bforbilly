## Foundation Syech Maulana Umar Masud

Transformative power of Foundation Yayasan Syekh Maulana Umar Masud. From staying informed about latest activities to achievement.
[Preview Is Limited]

### Features

- **Content Management System (CMS):** Website features a powerful content management system that enables seamless sourcing and organization of institutional content, ensuring that visitors have access to up-to-date and relevant information.
- **Article CRUD:** Easily create, update, and delete articles to share important news, announcements, and insights with our audience. Our intuitive interface simplifies the content creation process, allowing you to focus on delivering impactful messages.
- **PActivity Documentation Photos CRUD:** Capture and showcase the vibrant activities and events organized by our foundation with our activity documentation photo CRUD feature. Upload, manage, and curate photos to provide a visual narrative of our community engagements and initiatives.
- **Institutional Details CRUD:** Dive deeper into the essence of each institution within our foundation through our institutional details CRUD feature. Access comprehensive data on each institution, including name, social media links, principal information, achievements, staff members, and more.
- **IMonthly Work Program Photos CRUD:** Highlight the ongoing efforts and initiatives of our foundation with our monthly work program photo CRUD feature. Upload and manage photos that capture the essence of our monthly activities, displayed dynamically on a slider that changes according to the month.
- **Achievements Photos CRUD:** Celebrate our foundation's accomplishments and milestones with our achievements photo CRUD feature. Showcase photos that document our achievements and successes, inspiring our audience and fostering a sense of pride and accomplishment.

### Stack

- [Next](https://nextjs.org) - Next.js is a React framework for building full-stack web applications
- [TailwindCSS](https://tailwindcss.com) - Tailwind CSS: A utility-first CSS framework for versatile design.
- [Prisma](https://www.prisma.io/) - Prisma an open source tool that makes it fun. And safe to work with your database.
- [MySQL](https://www.mysql.com) - A relational database stores data in separate tables rather than putting all the data in one big storeroom.
