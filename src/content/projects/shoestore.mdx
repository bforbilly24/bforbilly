## Shoe Store: Your Destination for Footwear
E-Commerce website  and discover a world of possibilities at  fingertips. With  intuitive interface, stunning visuals, and user-friendly features. Exploring  and elevate online shopping experience.
[Source Code](https://github.com/bforbilly24/shoe-shop-codeigniter-fullstack-app).

### Features
- **User Accounts:** Create a personalized account on Shoe Store to track orders, save favorite items, and receive exclusive offers and discounts.
- **Effortless Shopping:** Easily add desired shoes to cart with just a few clicks. Intuitive interface ensures a smooth and hassle-free shopping experience.
- **Product Reviews:** Share thoughts and experiences with fellow shoppers by leaving reviews and ratings for the shoes purchased. Help others make informed decisions while earning rewards for contributions.
- **Order Management:** View and manage incoming orders, process payments, and update order statuses with ease.
- **Coupon Creation:** Generate discount coupons to attract customers and boost sales. Customize coupon codes, set expiration dates, and track their usage to maximize their effectiveness. 
- **Sales Analytics:** Gain valuable insights into your ShoeStore's performance with comprehensive sales analytics. Visualize sales data using interactive graphs and charts to identify trends and make informed business decisions.
- **Inventory Management:** Keep your inventory organized and up-to-date with ShoeStore's inventory management system. Add new products, update stock levels, and categorize items for easy navigation.
- **Customer Insights:** Get to know your customers better by analyzing their shopping behavior and preferences. Identify loyal customers, target specific demographics, and tailor your marketing strategies accordingly.
- **Feedback Management:** Monitor and respond to customer feedback to ensure satisfaction and maintain a positive reputation. Address any concerns or issues promptly to enhance the overall shopping experience for your customers.

### Stack
- [Codeigniter](https://www.codeigniter.com) - CodeIgniter is an free and open-source software rapid development web framework, for use in building dynamic web sites with PHP.
- [Bootstrap](https://getbootstrap.com) - Bootstrap is a free and open-source CSS framework directed at responsive, mobile-first front-end web development.
- [MySQL](https://www.mysql.com) - A relational database stores data in separate tables rather than putting all the data in one big storeroom.
- [SASS](https://sass-lang.com) - Sass is a preprocessor scripting language that is interpreted or compiled into Cascading Style Sheets.
- [PHP](https://www.php.net) - A popular general-purpose scripting language that is especially suited to web development.
