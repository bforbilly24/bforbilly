## Job Vacancy

Ultimate employment platform bridging the gap between talented job seekers and forward-thinking companies. This comprehensive recruitment solution streamlines the entire hiring process, from job discovery to application management, empowering both candidates and employers with powerful tools for successful career connections.

### Features

- **Comprehensive Job Search Engine:** Advanced search functionality enabling job seekers to explore diverse employment opportunities across multiple industries, with filtering options for experience level, location, salary range, and job type for personalized career discovery.

- **Intelligent Company Management:** Streamlined job posting and application management system for companies, featuring intuitive CRUD operations for job listings, candidate application tracking, and automated communication tools for efficient recruitment workflows.

- **Role-Based Access Control:** Sophisticated user management system with granular permissions for job seekers, companies, and administrators, ensuring secure data access and tailored platform experiences based on user roles and responsibilities.

- **Robust Administrative Dashboard:** Comprehensive admin control panel providing system-wide oversight of company profiles, job listings, user accounts, and platform analytics, enabling effective platform management and optimization.

- **Application Tracking System:** Complete candidate application management with status tracking, interview scheduling, and communication tools, streamlining the recruitment process from initial application to final hiring decisions.

- **Company Profile Management:** Detailed company profile system allowing employers to showcase their brand, culture, and career opportunities, attracting quality candidates through compelling organizational presentations.

### Stack

- [PHP](https://www.php.net) - Server-side scripting language designed for web development, providing robust backend functionality
- [MySQL](https://www.mysql.com) - Relational database management system for efficient data storage and retrieval operations
