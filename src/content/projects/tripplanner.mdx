### Features
- **Plan Your Adventures:** In addition to capturing memories and detecting locations, Trip Planner also allows you to create lists of places you want to visit. With this feature, you can easily organize your travel aspirations and turn them into actionable plans.
- **Snap and Save Memories Instantly:** Capture the beauty of your surroundings and save the moment forever with Trip Planner's built-in camera feature. Simply snap a photo of the breathtaking landscapes, iconic landmarks, or cherished moments during your travels.
- **Automatically Detect Locations:** Trip Planner goes beyond just capturing images â€“ it automatically detects the location where each photo was taken. Whether you're exploring a bustling cityscape or immersing yourself in nature's tranquility, Trip Planner ensures that every memory is accurately tagged with its location.
