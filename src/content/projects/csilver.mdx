## CSilver: Ultimate Job Search Platform
Whether a job seeker looking for your next career move or a company seeking skilled professionals, CSilver is here to streamline your journey to success.
[Source Code](https://github.com/bforbilly24/sim_job_arrangement).

### Features
- **Comprehensive Job Search:** Provides job seekers with a comprehensive search feature, allowing them to explore a vast array of employment opportunities tailored to their skills, experience, and preferences. From entry-level positions to executive roles, CSilver caters to professionals across various industries.
- **Effortless Company and Job Management:** Companies can effortlessly manage their job postings and applications through CSilver's intuitive platform. With simple CRUD operations, companies can create, update, and delete job listings, while also reviewing and responding to candidate applications with ease.
- **Role-based Access Control:** With role-based access control, CSilver ensures that each user type—job seekers, companies, and administrators—has access to relevant features and functionalities based on their roles. This granular control enhances security and usability, providing a tailored experience for every user.
- **Robust Admin Dashboard:**  Administrators have full control over CSilver's operations through a robust admin dashboard. From managing company profiles and job listings to overseeing user accounts and site settings, administrators can effectively monitor and optimize the platform's performance.

### Stack
- [PHP](https://www.php.net) - A popular general-purpose scripting language that is especially suited to web development.
- [MySQL](https://www.mysql.com) - A relational database stores data in separate tables rather than putting all the data in one big storeroom.
