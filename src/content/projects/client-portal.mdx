## Client Portal: Your All-in-One Client Management Solution
Experience the power of efficient client management with Client Portal.  Take your client management processes to the next level. From client details to project tracking and tutorials, Client Portal has everything you need to streamline your workflow and boost productivity.
[Preview](https://project-webcare.com/login), [Source Code](https://github.com/bforbilly24/client-portal-bootstrap-fullstack-app).

### Features
- **Carbon Footprint Calculator:** Navigate the filmmaking process seamlessly with Seco2's step-by-step guide to calculating the carbon footprint of your production.
- **Database of Carbon Emission Factors:** Access a wealth of data with Seco2's extensive database, covering carbon emission factors for various activities such as transportation, filming, and post-production.
- **Reporting Tool:** Track and showcase your progress in reducing the carbon footprint of your productions with Seco2's reporting tool.
- **Certification:** Homologated by the CNC, Seco2 meets the stringent requirements of the French government, offering essential certification for filmmakers seeking funding.

### Stack
- [Laravel](https://laravel.com) - Laravel is a free and open-source PHP web framework, created by Taylor Otwell and intended for the development of web applications following the model–view–controller architectural pattern and based on Symfony.
- [Bootstrap](https://getbootstrap.com) - Bootstrap is a free and open-source CSS framework directed at responsive, mobile-first front-end web development.
- [SASS](https://sass-lang.com) - Sass is a preprocessor scripting language that is interpreted or compiled into Cascading Style Sheets.
